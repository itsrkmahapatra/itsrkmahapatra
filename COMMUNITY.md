# Join Our Community

## Ways to Contribute
- **Report bugs:** Found a bug? Open an issue!
- **Suggest features:** Have a great idea? Let us know.
- **Write documentation:** Help us improve our docs.
- **Submit code:** Check out the `good first issue` labels.

## Communication Channels
- **GitHub Issues:** For bugs and features
- **GitHub Discussions:** For questions and ideas

## Community Guidelines
- Be respectful and welcoming.
- Help others learn and grow.
- Share knowledge openly.

## Support
If you need help, please check the GitHub Discussions tab or review our Documentation/Wiki.
